## Featured Projects

### [Lumen: Tagalog & Cebuano Bible](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.abantech.lumen)

A multilingual Bible application for reading and listening offline in Tagalog,
Cebuano, and English.

**Highlights:** Flutter, offline Bible audio, TTS, bookmarks, highlights,
reading progress, themes, widgets, and Google Play delivery.

### [OnDevice KYC Scanner](https://github.com/baclayonjonrel/OnDevice-KYC-Scanner)

A privacy-first SwiftUI document scanner with biometric access, live camera
guidance, on-device OCR, encrypted records, and protected document images.

**Highlights:** SwiftUI, AVFoundation, Apple Vision, TensorFlow Lite/LiteRT,
PP-OCR, LocalAuthentication, Realm encryption, Keychain, and file protection.

### [Mini Live Chat](https://github.com/baclayonjonrel/Mini-Live-Chat)

A full-stack realtime communication platform supporting text messaging,
video calls, AI-generated meeting notes, and live speech transcription.

**Highlights:** Swift, WebRTC, SkyWay, Socket.IO, Node.js, MongoDB, Gemini AI,
and Speech-to-Text.

### [ChatbotAI](https://github.com/baclayonjonrel/chatbot)

A mobile AI chatbot that connects a native iOS client to realtime messaging
services and locally hosted language models.

**Highlights:** Swift, UIKit, Socket.IO, Express, MongoDB, Ollama, LLaMA,
Mistral, and Markdown rendering.
